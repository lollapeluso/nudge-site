The Nudge philosophy

Consistency matters more than intensity

A lasting fitness habit does not require every day to include a perfect workout. Small amounts of movement, repeated consistently, can make a meaningful difference.

Nudge is designed to make the next small action obvious. It does not ask you to train harder. It helps you keep moving.

Apple Health integration

Nudge turns your Apple Health step data into a daily plan

With your permission, Nudge reads step count data from Apple Health to calculate your progress, remaining steps, estimated walking time, historical trends, and walk recommendations.

Nudge does not create a separate step count. It uses the information already collected by your iPhone and Apple Watch, then presents it in a way that is easier to act on.

Frequently asked questions

Questions about Nudge

What is Nudge?

Nudge is an Apple Watch and iPhone companion that helps you reach your daily step goal. It shows your remaining steps, estimated walking time, pace, reminders, and practical walk recommendations.

Is Nudge a step tracker?

Nudge uses step data collected through Apple Health rather than creating a separate step count. It then turns that data into a daily plan.

Does Nudge replace Apple Workouts?

No. Apple Workouts records individual walks, runs, hikes, and workouts. Nudge complements it by helping you finish your overall daily step goal.

Can Nudge help me reach 10,000 steps?

Yes. You can choose a 10,000-step daily goal or set another target that better fits your routine. Nudge shows how many steps and walking minutes remain.

How can I reach my daily step goal more consistently?

Breaking a large goal into smaller walking sessions can make it easier to complete. Nudge helps by showing remaining steps, estimated walking time, and practical walk recommendations throughout the day.

Does Nudge calculate walking time?

Yes. Nudge estimates the walking time needed to complete your remaining steps using your selected walking pace.

Can I customize walking reminders?

Yes. Nudge includes optional Daily Nudges, progress reminders, walk recommendations, and goal celebrations that can be adjusted to fit your routine.

Does Nudge include Apple Watch complications?

Yes. Nudge includes Apple Watch complications that make your remaining steps and daily progress visible from supported watch faces.

Does Nudge work with Apple Health?

Yes. With your permission, Nudge reads step data from Apple Health to calculate progress, remaining steps, walking-time estimates, and coaching guidance.

Does Nudge work on older iPhones and Apple Watches?

Nudge supports compatible iPhones running iOS 17 or later and compatible Apple Watches running watchOS 10 or later.

Does Nudge track my location?

Nudge is focused on step progress and daily walking guidance. For details about how data is handled, review the Nudge Privacy Policy.

How accurate are Nudge step counts?

Nudge displays step data provided by Apple Health. Results can vary based on which Apple devices you carry or wear, how Apple Health combines data sources, and when step information is refreshed.
